# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Academic economists, potential collaborators, graduate admissions committees, and policy researchers who need to understand Miguel Del Valle’s research agenda, access current papers, and find reliable contact information quickly.

## Product Purpose

Present a clear, credible research identity and make working papers, the CV, and references straightforward to find and maintain.

## Positioning

An economist who combines disaggregated economic accounts with frontier quantitative models to study the aggregate and distributional consequences of economic disruption and policy.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary action: read a working paper or download the CV.
- Secondary action: contact Miguel Del Valle or visit a professional profile.
- Visitor takeaway: a precise, research-led profile rather than a generic personal portfolio.
- Proof on hand: one submitted working paper, professional biography, CV, and named academic references.

## Brand Personality

Measured, rigorous, and quietly distinctive. The experience should feel scholarly and contemporary without becoming commercial, technological, or ornamental.

## Anti-references

Startup landing pages, generic developer portfolios, dense card grids, invented metrics, glassmorphism, decorative gradients, and excessive motion.

## Design Principles

1. Research first: papers and their underlying claims receive visual priority.
2. Editorial restraint: typography, proportion, and real imagery carry recognition.
3. Accessible by default: motion, contrast, focus, and mobile navigation are intentional.
4. Update without rewriting: research and references live in structured data.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, semantic landmarks, sufficient contrast, responsive layouts, and reduced motion preferences.
